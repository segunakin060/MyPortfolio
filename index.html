Background

I am Segun Akinsoyinu, a cybersecurity practitioner currently pursuing an MSc in Cybersecurity at Coventry University. This portfolio documents my practical penetration testing work completed as part of the 7072SCN Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing module.

Across three isolated VMware lab environments, I executed complete attack chains from unauthenticated network access to full root shell, documenting every vulnerability with CVSS v3.1 scores, Cyber Kill Chain mapping, and professional-grade remediation guidance.

My background spans AI evaluation and content quality assessment, compliance-adjacent analysis, and customer service operations, giving me both the technical depth and the communication skills to translate complex security findings into clear, actionable language for non-technical stakeholders.
